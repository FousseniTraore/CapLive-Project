About CapLive

CapLive is a web application that allows users to track and manage their vehicles in real-time. With CapLive, you can easily monitor your vehicles, view their location on a map, and receive notifications when certain events occur, such as when a vehicle enters or leaves a specific area. CapLive is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive, so you can get started quickly and easily.
